F1, or Formula One, is not just a popular global motorsport, but it can also be considered as a hobby. Many people around the world enjoy watching F1 races and following their favorite drivers and teams. For some, it's not just about the speed and adrenaline rush, but the excitement of the race, the strategy, and the technical aspects that make F1 so fascinating. People who love F1 as a hobby often keep up-to-date with the latest news, attend races when possible, collect merchandise, and engage in discussions about the sport. Some even take it a step further and participate in simulations, where they can experience the thrill of driving an F1 car virtually. All in all, F1 can be a fun and engaging hobby for those who have a passion for motorsport and the world of racing.

Max Verstappen is a Dutch racing driver who competes in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. He was born on September 30, 1997, in Hasselt, Belgium, and began karting at a young age. Verstappen made his debut in Formula One in 2015, becoming the youngest driver in the sport's history at just 17 years old. Since then, he has become one of the most exciting and successful drivers in the sport, known for his aggressive and fearless driving style. In 2021, Verstappen won his first World Championship title, becoming the first Dutch driver to do so. He has won numerous races and pole positions, and has often been praised for his exceptional car control and overtaking abilities. Verstappen is considered one of the sport's brightest talents and is expected to continue to make waves in the world of Formula One for years to come.

Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver who competes in Formula One for Mercedes-AMG Petronas. He was born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England, and began karting at the age of eight. Hamilton made his debut in Formula One in 2007, becoming the first Black driver to race in the sport. Since then, he has become one of the most successful drivers in Formula One history, winning seven World Championship titles and numerous races and pole positions. Hamilton is known for his incredible talent behind the wheel, his speed, and his ability to consistently perform at the highest level. He is also an advocate for social justice and equality, using his platform to raise awareness of issues such as racism and environmentalism. Hamilton's success on and off the track has made him a role model for many young people around the world, and he is considered one of the greatest drivers of all time.
